WHAT IS SMART?

SMART® is a budgeting cost recovery tool system focused on optimizing enterprise budgeting and ERP systems by simplifying business implementations. This intern promotes for cost effectively maximizing finance and accounting production time through a strategic method called Recovery Management. Recovery Management is a mechanism that is used to charge and recover values for a multitude of internal services, using charge and recovery vehicles known as absorption and consumption drivers. These absorption and consumption drivers are modelled to identify and group expense values allocated to internal operations which SMART® uses to dynamically transact between internal and external subsidiaries or business entities, and can be specifically tailored to optimize any organizational accounting structure. This means that SMART has the ability to adapt to any complex business process by effectively simplifying its internal mapping structures through granular budgeting. SMART® achieves this by integrating operational data with Service Modelling, measuring business workflows and planning strategies with Expense Modelling, and tying these elements into one central base for optimizing business efficiency. SMART® provides its end users with accurate and immediate access into its organisational budgeting data in real – time, allowing the end users to perform effective decision making and reporting throughout a set financial period.
