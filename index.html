OUR KEY FUNCTIONS

AT OIS we take Tech and Finance seriously. Thus building the most flexible, secure and affordable business solutions.

IAM - Asset Management

The intranet Asset Management system is effectively a financial asset register, which is fully, integrated into the various iw@re application suite asset tracking functions. More specifically these functions are: iPS asset tagging providing the tracking of an asset through its full asset life cycle, the iPM problem requests logged and costed against the tagged item and the iPS used stock module which records the assets final decommissioning. This will provide the client with the total cost of ownership for the asset from "cradle to grave".

IBUD - Budget Generator

The intranet Budget Generator system allows for the history of all transactions captured via the iw@re application suite to be collated and summarised per cost center, per month so as to generate an initial budget template. This pre-populated historical cost template can then be further manipulated through ''scenario planning tools''. The system will allow for integration into reference data sources for those costs that were not initiated through iw@re systems, such as the HR costs of salaries, pensions etc.

ICC - Change Control

The intranet Change Control system is a change request logging system and slot booking system. It has an intranet-based client interface, which can be accessed by all employees, or other relevant parties. This front-end will display schedules of existing slot bookings, and allow for the application of new slot bookings to be submitted for approval and authorisation. The system does not only cater for IT oriented changes but also provides for slot booking for other services such as the painting of offices, large office move projects, corporate projects etc.

The history about our successful iw@re products.

The iw@re application suite was developed by a highly qualified and motivated team of professionals in their quest to find practical solutions to actual client legacy problems.

A significant number of integrated business solutions have been developed over many years culminating into the iware application suite. During this period we found increased evidence that the B2C (business to client) and B2B (business to business) initiatives were often very lengthy and complicated to implement and therefore often inversely proportionate to the benefits that our clients were in pursuit of from e-commerce. It was just not delivering real bottom line value to our clients.
